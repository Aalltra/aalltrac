Bunjilaka Aboriginal Cultural Centre

The museum is on land where Aboriginal people have lived, laughed, fought, married, dreamed and died for thousands of years. Now, in Bunjilaka, we tell the story of survival against the odds and celebrate our vibrant cultures through performances, storytelling, artwork and more.

Opening Hours

Daily: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location

Melbourne Museum Nicholson St, Bunjilaka Aboriginal Cultural Centre, Carlton VIC 3053

Admission

Included with Melbourne Getaway Package

Melbourne Zoo

The Royal Melbourne Zoological Gardens (official name: Royal Melbourne Zoological Gardens) is an Australian zoo located in the state of Victoria, in Melbourne. It features 460 species of animals from around the world and more particularly from Australia.

Opening Hours

Daily: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location

Elliott Avenue, Parkville Victoria 3052

Admission

Included with Melbourne Getaway Package

Birrarung Marr Park

Located on the northern bank of the Yarra River between Federation Square and Melbourne Park, Birrarung Marr features a range of native flora and sculpted terraces. It is also the location for the children's arts centre, ArtPlay, and provides space for many events held throughout the year.

Opening Hours

Daily: 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Location

Batman Avenue, Melbourne Victoria 3000

Admission

Included with Melbourne Getaway Package
